Show-Me Star & Snowglobes

When the Star is sparkling, click on it to show all pictures briefly. It will recharge over time. It can only be used when no images have been selected.

The snowglobes will show you possible matches with the ornament you have selected. Can only be activated once an ornament is selected.

Difficulty levels are:
1 - Basic memory game, 5 snowglobes and Show-Me star are activated
2 - Add in random snowballs that will cover up partial pictures, 4 snowglobes
3 - Snowballs have a higher chance of occuring more often, 3 snowglobes
4 - Along with snowballs, the tree will rotate at random, 2 snowglobes
5- BOTH Snowballs and Tree rotation happen more often, 1 snowglobe
